Rosenbluth

class Rosenbluth

Store the energies and Rosenbluth factors necessary for configurational bias Monte Carlo trials.

\(w = \frac{1}{n} \sum_i^n \exp^{-\beta \Delta U}\)

where \(w\) is the Rosenbluth weight for \(n\) steps.
