Action

class Action

An Action is for use with the MonteCarlo(arglist) constructor. Actions are performed immediately when they reach the top of the arglist. Examples include modifications to the simulations, or the initiation of a run.

Subclassed by feasst::ActionExample, feasst::ConvertToRefPotential, feasst::RefPotential, feasst::RemoveAnalyze, feasst::RemoveModify, feasst::RemoveTrial, feasst::Run, feasst::WriteCheckpoint, feasst::WriteStepper
